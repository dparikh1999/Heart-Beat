What is HeartBeat?

HeartBeat is an interactive playlist generator which creates Spotify playlists according to the user's mood. Music is an integral part of human experience and shows a strong correlation to one's mood and mental health. Listening to music in alignment with one's mood can act as a source of pleasure and comfort. In addition to psychological comfort, listening to music eliciting certain feelings triggers positive changes in autoimmune and neuroendocrine activity and can even reduce stress. HeartBeat's purpose is to provide users with a range of music harmonious with their current mental state.
